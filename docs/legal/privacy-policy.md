# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 2024

## 1. Introduction

Oppskrift ("we", "our", "the service") is a federated recipe sharing platform. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is the instance administrator of this Oppskrift server.

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account Data
- Username (required)
- Display name (optional)
- Email address (if provided)
- Profile biography (optional)
- Avatar image (optional)
- Measurement preferences (metric/imperial)

### 3.2 Content Data
- Recipes you create (title, description, ingredients, instructions, images)
- Recipe books and collections
- Comments and interactions

### 3.3 Technical Data
- IP address (for security and rate limiting)
- Browser user agent
- Access timestamps
- ActivityPub federation identifiers

### 3.4 Cryptographic Data
- RSA public/private keypairs for ActivityPub HTTP Signatures

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases (GDPR Article 6):

| Data Category | Legal Basis | Purpose |
|---------------|-------------|---------|
| Account data | Contract performance | Provide the service |
| Content data | Contract performance | Enable recipe sharing |
| Technical data | Legitimate interest | Security, abuse prevention |
| Cryptographic keys | Contract performance | Federation authentication |

## 5. How We Use Your Data

- **Service delivery**: Display your profile and recipes
- **Federation**: Share content with other ActivityPub servers you interact with
- **Security**: Protect against unauthorized access and abuse
- **Communication**: Send service-related notifications

## 6. Data Sharing

### 6.1 Federation
When you interact with users on other ActivityPub servers, your public profile and content are shared with those servers according to the ActivityPub protocol.

### 6.2 Third Parties
We do not sell your data. We may share data with:
- Infrastructure providers (hosting, storage) under data processing agreements
- Law enforcement when legally required

## 7. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion |
| Content data | Until content/account deletion |
| Security logs | 90 days |
| Backup data | 30 days after deletion |

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR Articles 15-22)

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Delete your account and data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent**: Where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact the instance administrator.

## 9. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure password hashing
- RSA cryptographic signatures for federation
- Regular security audits
- Access logging and monitoring

## 10. International Transfers

If your data is transferred outside the EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions).

## 11. Cookies

We use essential cookies only for:
- Session management
- Security tokens (CSRF protection)

No tracking or analytics cookies are used.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced on the platform. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 13. Contact

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your rights, contact the instance administrator.

For complaints, you may also contact your local data protection authority.
