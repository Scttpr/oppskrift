About Oppskrift

What is Oppskrift?

Oppskrift (Norwegian for "recipe") is a federated social platform for sharing recipes. Built on the ActivityPub protocol, it allows users to share their culinary creations with people across the fediverse.

Features

  • Recipe Sharing - Create detailed recipes with ingredients, instructions, and photos
  • Recipe Books - Organize recipes into themed collections
  • Federation - Connect with users on other ActivityPub-compatible platforms
  • Privacy-Focused - Your data stays on your instance
  • Open Source - Fully open source under AGPL-3.0

Federation

Oppskrift uses the ActivityPub protocol, the same technology that powers Mastodon, PeerTube, and other federated platforms. This means you can follow users and discover recipes from other Oppskrift instances and compatible platforms.

Open Source

Oppskrift is free and open source software, licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0. You can view the source code, contribute, or run your own instance.

Technology

Built with:

  • Rust & Axum for the backend
  • PostgreSQL for data storage
  • HTMX for dynamic interactions
  • Tailwind CSS for styling
  • ActivityPub for federation
