When enabled, your profile and public recipes can be discovered and followed by users on other ActivityPub-compatible platforms (such as Mastodon, Lemmy, or other Oppskrift instances).

When disabled: Your profile is hidden from federation. Existing followers on other instances will no longer receive your updates.
